Video & Article 24 April 2026

NVC: you get to keep your swear words!!!

When people hear "Nonviolent Communication" they often assume it's about suppression — becoming blander, more controlled, never raising your voice. In this conversation with Amy Eleftheriades, founder of CHMLN, we explore why that's exactly backwards. NVC isn't about getting rid of your strong judgements. It's about going through them rather than around them — and coming out the other side with something useful. Amy even introduced me to a beautiful ancient Greek word I'd never heard before.
