In-person NVC Foundation training is effective, efficient, and less and less frequent in London. This is a rare opportunity to leave the screens behind and learn NVC with others in the room. My training style is fun and participatory, with an emphasis on practice. No prerequisites are required for this training.

Nonviolent Communication fosters empathy and understanding in our interactions with family, friends and workmates. This approach provides a subtle yet powerful framework to get to the essence of any issue and support effective communication.

What this course will support you to do

  • Hone your empathy skills when listening to family, friends and workmates
  • Identify your values — the things that really matter to you
  • Ask for the things that matter to you without upsetting others
  • Develop your all-around skill and ability as an effective communicator

Carolyn Davies is a Certified Trainer with the Center for Nonviolent Communication. A filmmaker by profession, she is passionate about the potential NVC has to transform relationships with family, friends and work colleagues.

Carolyn works closely with London Certified Trainers — Cath Burke, Ceri Buckmaster, Daren De Witt, and Tracy Seed. Together we are the NVC Pathway Partners, a collaborative group of London-based CNVC Certified Trainers committed to creating a community and supportive network for those learning, living and sharing NVC in London.
