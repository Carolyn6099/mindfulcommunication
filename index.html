Are you tired of conversations that go nowhere? Frustrated by conflict that keeps repeating? NVC gives you a practical framework to express yourself honestly, listen deeply and resolve what's really going on — at home, at work, and within yourself.

  • Communicate your needs without the drama
  • Build deeper, more satisfying relationships

Your Trainer

Carolyn Davies — CNVC Certified Trainer

I came to NVC through a long career in television — producing and directing for the ABC, BBC and ITV — where I discovered that most of the hardest problems on set weren't technical. They were human.

In 2004 I found Nonviolent Communication and it changed everything. I became a CNVC Certified Trainer in 2014 and have been delivering workshops and coaching ever since — for individuals, teams, schools, charities and corporates across London and online.

My training style is warm, participatory and grounded in real practice. People leave not just knowing about NVC — they leave being able to use it.
