What I Offer

Training that fits your organisation

Poor communication costs organisations — in productivity, staff wellbeing and retention. NVC gives teams a shared language for navigating difficulty, disagreement and change.

I design each programme around your context — your sector, your people and the specific challenges you're facing. Sessions can be delivered as a one-day workshop, a series of shorter sessions, or an ongoing programme.

Delivered in person in London, across the UK and in Europe — and online via Zoom worldwide.
