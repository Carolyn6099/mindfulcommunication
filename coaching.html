How Coaching Works

Support tailored to you

I offer empathetic listening and coaching using the NVC framework. If there's something you're struggling with — a relationship conflict, communication patterns at work, difficulty expressing yourself — I can help.

Sessions are available face to face in London (Clapham) or online via Zoom. I want to make sure that the NVC coaching I offer matches your needs, so please do get in touch first so we can have a brief conversation about what you're looking for.

Coaching can support you to:

  • Navigate a specific conflict or difficult relationship
  • Develop your empathy and listening skills
  • Communicate more clearly and assertively
  • Break unhelpful patterns of anger, guilt or resentment
  • Process difficult feelings with care and clarity
  • Prepare for a challenging conversation

Who I Work With

Is coaching right for you?

Coaching works well for anyone who wants personalised support — whether you're dealing with a specific situation or wanting to deepen your NVC practice more generally.

People who typically come to coaching are:

  • Experiencing recurring conflict with a colleague, partner or family member
  • Struggling to express their needs without triggering defensiveness
  • Finding themselves caught in patterns of anger, withdrawal or people-pleasing
  • Wanting to develop their empathy and emotional intelligence
  • Preparing for a high-stakes or difficult conversation
  • NVC practitioners wanting to deepen their skills through practice
